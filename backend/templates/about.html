📈 Sales Analysis

Explore every dimension of your sales data

The sales page gives you an 8-parameter filter system — category, region, date range, and price bounds. Every chart updates in real time as you filter, pulling freshly aggregated data from the Flask backend on each query. Monthly aggregation keeps charts clean and readable.

🔮 Predictive Analytics

Linear regression trained on your own data

A separate linear regression model is fit for each category plus one for overall revenue. Each model learns the trend from historical monthly data and extends it 6 months forward. Solid lines show history, dashed lines show forecast — all on a shared x-axis with a checkbox filter per category.

scikit-learn Linear Regression Monthly aggregation Seasonality detection

🎯 Decision Support

Goal-seeking optimisation with price elasticity

You define a target — say, +20% revenue — and the system trains a log-log regression to measure how price-sensitive each category is. An optimiser then searches all valid price and discount combinations to find the one that moves you closest to your goal without breaking your margin floor.

Scenario simulation at your fingertips

Three sliders — selling price, cost price, volume — let you construct any scenario. Apply changes to all categories or just selected ones. The backend instantly recalculates all KPIs against your original baseline and auto-generates insight flags for risks like margin compression.
